Wardell Stephen Curry II

is an American professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time and the greatest shooter in NBA history, Curry has been named the NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP) twice and won three NBA championships with the Warriors. He is the son of former NBA player Dell Curry and older brother of current NBA player Seth Curry.
